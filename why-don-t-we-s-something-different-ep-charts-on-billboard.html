Why Don't We's 'Something Different' EP Charts on Billboard

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Why Don’t We are one of the hottest boyband’s around at the moment and they are killing it with their debut EP ‘Something Different‘. The five-piece group are currently signed to Signature Entertainment and their EP was released back last month.

Since its official release ‘Something Different’ has been gaining a lot of exposure and this past week their efforts were there for everyone to see thanks to the Billboard chart in the US. The lads debuted at number 13 on the Independent Albums Chart and number 6 on the Heatseeker Albums Chart.

Their chart success hasn’t ended there though. Along with the success of their EP, Why Don’t We also reached number 2 on Billboard’s Next Big Sound chart. And over on iTunes, ‘Something Different’ debuted at number 2 on the Pop Albums chart and  number 13 on the Top Albums chart. Their latest single also titled ‘Something Different’ has also reached number 38 on Spotify’s US Viral 50 Chart.

For the official music video for their latest track the boys teamed up with social media powerhouse Logan Paul. Since its release back in April the clip has gained over 2 million views on YouTube and also reached number 15 on the sites’ Trending Videos page upon its release.
