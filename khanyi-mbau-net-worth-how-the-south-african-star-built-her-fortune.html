Khanyi Mbau is one of the most famous and successful celebrities in South Africa.

Who is Khanyi Mbau?

Khanyi Mbau was born on October 15, 1985, in Soweto, Johannesburg.

She was nurtured by her maternal grandparents and embarked on her acting journey at a tender age.

Khanyi graced TV commercials for esteemed brands like Coca-Cola, Ponds, and L’Oreal.

Her debut in the soap opera Muvhango, as Doobsie, occurred in 2004.

Following this, she showcased her talent in renowned soap operas such as Mzansi, Generations, and Uzalo.

Additionally, she took the helm of various TV shows, including The Scoop, Katch It With Khanyi, and Whose Show Is It Anyway?

Not merely confined to acting and hosting, Khanyi Mbau wears many hats; she is a singer, author, and entrepreneur.

Her eponymous debut album, released in 2010, featured tracks like Shake and La ‘Wey.

In 2012, she penned her autobiography, “Bitch Please! I’m Khanyi Mbau,” offering an in-depth look into her personal and professional life.

She presides over a beauty sanctuary named I Am Khanyi – Millennial Shimmer Gin, and has aligned herself with distinguished products and brands, such as Virgin Mobile and Black Opal Cosmetics.

Khanyi Mbau’s private life hasn’t escaped the glare of media scrutiny and controversy.

Her union with businessman Mandla Mthembu, from 2006 to 2009, was marked by opulence, earning her the moniker “Queen of Bling.” Subsequently, she shared a romantic journey with businessman Tebogo Lerole from 2011 to 2018.

Presently, she is entwined in a relationship with Zimbabwean magnate Kudzai Mushonga, who is sought by authorities for purported fraudulent activities.
