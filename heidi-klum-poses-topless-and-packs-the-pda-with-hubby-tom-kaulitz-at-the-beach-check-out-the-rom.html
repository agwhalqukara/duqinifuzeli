Heidi Klum Poses Topless And Packs The PDA With Hubby Tom Kaulitz At The Beach Check Out The Rom

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 27, 2024

Heidi Klum and Tom Kaulitz have been husband and wife for the past two years and it looks like they are still just as in love as ever! Everything is going great and this was made obvious by their latest post on social media!

The married couple can be seen packing the PDA on a beach while enjoying the sunny weather and each other’s company of course!

The model looked like she was as happy as she can be while sharing a sweet kiss with her man.

The pictures were taken at sunset and while locking lips, the couple also created the two halves of a heart with their hands to frame the reddish run setting in the background.

With the other hand, Heidi covered her exposed top part since she was seemingly sunbathing without a bikini bra on at the time.

All in all, the image of the couple kissing, forming a hand heart together and just bathing in the sunset glow while their hair was swaying in the breeze was super aesthetic!

Not to mention that the married couple was also wearing matching black bathing suits!

Of course, the romantic selfie is not something new for them since Heidi and Tom have made a habit of documenting some of their sweetest PDA moments and sharing them on social media with their followers!

Whether they’re kissing, cuddling, or expressing their feelings in any other way, they always look super in love and happy to have one another in each other’s lives!

Now that summer is upon us, it’s safe to assume that the couple will be sharing even more such romantic pictures of them on such outings, packing the PDA and just making the internet as a whole, jealous!
