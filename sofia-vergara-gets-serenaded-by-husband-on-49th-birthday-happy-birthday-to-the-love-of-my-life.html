Sofia Vergara gets serenaded by husband on 49th birthday:'Happy birthday to the love of my life!'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

Sofia Vergara is celebrating her 49th birthday with family and friends at her secret oasis in the Caribbean that she likes to call ‘Casa Chipi Chipi.’

And her husband Joe Manganiello, taking a break from vacation activities, took to Instagram to share a heartfelt message of love, including snaps of the Modern Family star on her special day.

‘¡Feliz Cumpleaños al amor de mi vida!’ the True Blood star, 44, wrote to his wife of more than five-years in the caption, which translates in English to ‘Happy birthday to the love of my life!’

In the first photo, the Modern Family alum can be seen sitting in front of a large window inside their Caribbean hideaway with the gorgeous greenery and ocean in the backdrop.

Sofia relished in the beautiful view, while decked out in a black spaghetti-strap dress and brown platform heels.

Looking over her right shoulder in a classic pose, Vergara had her light brown tresses styled long and flowing with a part in the middle.

Serenading: Vergara’s husband of more than five years, Joe Manganiello, paid tribute to his leading lady by sharing a series of images of the Modern Family alum on Instagram

Manganiello’s snaps of Sofia gave fans a glimpse at the star’s globetrotting life, including waiting in line to view Leonardo da Vinci’s famed Mona Lisa painting that’s being displayed The Louvre Museum in Paris, France. 

The Magic Mike star also shared a snap of the couple sharing a passionate kiss; Vergara soaking in another gorgeous view; the sun’s glare taking a co-starring role in a photo of the actress sitting on a bench; the Colombian native seemingly leaving a hotel with the lovely blue ocean in the background; and Vergara flashing a seductive smile while enjoying her time in a cafe.

There’s also a photo of the couple snuggled up together under the glaring sun, with both of them wearing stylish sunglasses.

Mother Nature’s artwork: The proud husband also shared a couple of gorgeous views: the Colombian native seemingly leaving a hotel and  the lovely blue ocean in the backdrop

Cup of Joe: Vergara flashed a seductive smile while enjoying her time in a cafe in another pic

Vergara also gave her 22.2 million Instagram fans and followers a glimpse at the festive celebration: first by sharing a photo of herself wearing a purple bodysuit that she paired with a yellow skirt-wrap. 

She also donned cool sunglasses and had an added touch of volume to her locks for the snap, which was taken on a beach just feet from the incoming tide.

‘Bday day!! #casachipichipi,’ the actress wrote in the caption of her own birthday post, along with seashell and crab emojis.

Of course, her special day wouldn’t be complete without a proper Happy Birthday song and cake, delivered by some of the people closest to her.

In a video posted on her Insta-Story, the America’s Got Talent judge can be seen laying on the floor of her idyllic retreat as family and friends kicked into the opening lines of Happy Birthday.

Before the end of the song, she struggles to get up on her feet for a brief moment, but eventually she makes it up and to grab a pink cake, which led to everyone going into a Spanish version of Happy Birthday.  

Her son Manolo Gonzalez Vergara, 29, from her first marriage to high school sweetheart, Joe Gonzalez, shared a clip of the family celebration. 

‘Happy birthday to the most graceful mom ever!! Love you @sofiavergara,’ he joked in a caption, in a reference to her struggle to get up from the floor during Happy Birthday.

Hilarious: The Modern Family star made everyone laugh when she had a brief problem getting off the floor of her idyllic retreat during her birthday song

Mission accomplished! Eventually, Vergara made it to her feet for a Spanish version of Happy Birthday

The previous day, Vergara gave the newest member of her family members, her dog Bubbles, the five-star treatment on her birthday on Friday, which was documented with a series of snaps.

‘Its Bubbles bday today!!! Baguette,Petunia and Bubbles wearing their @caninibybaguette,’ the proud mama wrote about the Chihuahua/Pomeranian mix, and her four-legged siblings, along with a reference to their animal print ensembles. 

One photo showed Bubbles as she got ready to take a bite out of a tiny birthday cake decorated with pink roses and a flamingo garnish.     

Vergara and Manganiello got engaged just six months after their first date on Christmas Day 2014.

They would go on to tie the knot less than a year later, in November 2015, in a ceremony in Palm Beach, Florida.
