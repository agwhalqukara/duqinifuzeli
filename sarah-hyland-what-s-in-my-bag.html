Sarah Hyland: What's In My Bag?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

Nothing to sneeze at! Sarah Hyland is always prepared for a pollen attack. “I carry tons and tons of tissues because of my bad allergies,” says the star of Modern Family (Season 7 premieres on ABC Sept. 23 at 9 p.m). “I’m like a grandma.” The New York native, 24, who dates British actor Dominic Sherwood, 25, lets Us Weekly nose around her Lady Dior tote.

Less Is More

“I don’t wear a lot of makeup when I’m not at work, but I need my Diorshow Iconic Overcurl mascara. It gives my lashes the most amazing length. And I love NARS bronzer and Aquaphor lip repair. I used to carry a bottle of black nail polish, but it spilled everywhere!”

Take Note

“Writing in my leather journal is my creative outlet. I like to sketch things and write songs.”

Head Turner

“I have a big bottle of Oribe texturizing spray. I love the way it makes my hair smell. And I’ve always got hair ties floating around. I only use the metal-free kind.”

Medicine Woman

“I’m the local pharmacy. I never leave home without a bottle of Tylenol Extra Strength and Emergen-C.”

Thank You!

You have successfully subscribed.

Novel Idea

“I just bought The Goldfinch by Donna Tartt. I haven’t had any time to start it yet! I’ve just been very, very busy.”

More of what’s in Hyland’s bag: passport, cinnamon roll Quest Protein Bar, cell phone, sunglasses, Hawaiian Tropic sunscreen spray, American Express credit card, almonds, and raisins.
