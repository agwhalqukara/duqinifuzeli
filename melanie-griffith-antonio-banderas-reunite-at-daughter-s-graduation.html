Melanie Griffith, Antonio Banderas Reunite at Daughter's Graduation

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 23, 2024

Proud of their baby girl! Exes Melanie Griffith and Antonio Banderas put up a united front as they attended their daughter Stella's high school graduation on Sunday, June 7. The Day Out of Days actress, who filed for divorce from Banderas exactly one year ago, posted family photos from the special day via Instagram.

"Mama, Stella and Papi. Our angel is amazing," Griffith, 57, captioned one snapshot of the trio. "Beginning the next chapter of her life! Freedom!!" 

All eyes weren't just on Stella, though. Griffith noticeably covered up her famous "Antonio" tattoo while wearing a sleeveless summer dress. She began laser treatments to have it removed last July.

The Now and Then star also posted individual photos of herself and Banderas with their drop-dead gorgeous offspring. "Stella and Mama at Graduation luncheon #proudmama," she wrote. In a second, she gushed: "Father and daughter celebrating at Baccalaureate luncheon! So sweet!!"

Griffith and the Expendables 3 actor, 54, went their separate ways after 19 years of marriage in June 2014. "There was no cheating on either side. They had fallen out of love awhile ago," a source told Us Weekly at the time.

Griffith is also mom to Fifty Shades of Grey actress Dakota Johnson, whom she shares with ex Don Johnson, and son Alexander, whom she had with actor Steven Bauer.

"Me and my fabulous kids! Stella, Alexander, and Dakota," Griffith captioned another photo on Sunday. "My joy, my heart, my life is theirs."
