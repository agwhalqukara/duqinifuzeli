Hollywood Film Awards 2014: Complete Winners List Includes Gone Girl

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 19, 2024

Shailene, Benedict, Julianne, oh my! The 2014 Hollywood Film Awards may have been filled with nip slips and flubs, but stars like Shailene Woodley and newly engaged Benedict Cumberbatch still had their moment on stage in front of their peers on Friday, Nov. 14.

In addition, other notable films that were recognized included Ben Affleck's Gone Girl, and Chris Pratt's Guardians of the Galaxy.
