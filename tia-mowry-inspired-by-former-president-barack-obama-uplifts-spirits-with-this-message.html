Tia Mowry, Inspired By Former President Barack Obama, Uplifts Spirits With This Message

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 26, 2024

Tia Mowry is still practicing isolation in her California mansion with her family.

She has been doing her best to keep things happy and fun. Still, sometimes the current events get a bit heavy with all that is going on in the world, including the coronavirus and the social unrest due to racism and police brutality.

The Sister Sister star recently posted a picture in a cute outfit and used a quote from former President Barack Obama to uplift spirits.

https://www.instagram.com/p/CAsS7ksFIs6/

Tia wrote: “Change will not come if we wait for some other person or some other time. We are the ones we’ve been waiting for. We are the change that we seek- Barack Obama 🙏🏽 #change #itstime.”

She later added: “Taking a breath. It’s needed. It’s really needed. My emotions have been so up and down this year. One minute I feel like I can handle things, and the next, I’m overwhelmed with #anxiety and #fear and #sadness about what’s going on in our world. But then, I take a moment and take a deep breath and literally mediate on the #positives. I say all this to say, please take care of your #mentalhealth during these trying times. It’s important. 🙏🏽 #meditation #breath”

https://www.instagram.com/p/CBfxOV1jGg6/
