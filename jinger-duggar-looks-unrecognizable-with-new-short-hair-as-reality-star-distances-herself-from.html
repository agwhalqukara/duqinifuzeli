Jinger Duggar looks unrecognizable with new short hair as reality star distances herself from

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 24, 2024

JINGER Duggar switched up her look and debuted a short new haircut in a pretty selfie.

The Counting On star recently admitted she “needed change” before chopping off her long locks.

Jinger, 27, looked glamorous in a new selfie that her husband Jeremy, 33, shared on his Instagram page.

The reality star showed off her shoulder-length haircut tied back with a small clip, as she smiled in a bright blue dress that matched her eye color.

Jeremy gushed over his wife as he wrote in the caption: “More selfies please ?.”

The couple tied the knot in 2016 and share two kids, Felicity, three, and Evangeline, eight months.

NEED FOR ‘CHANGE’

Over the weekend, Jinger debuted her new style as she claimed she needed some “change” following the dramatic news of her brother Josh’s arrest.

The TV personality showed off her blunt new style in a series of photos as she posed outdoors.

“Sometimes you just need to change the hair up a bit,” she captioned her update.

Despite her ultra-conservative family’s rule of girls keeping their hair long, some of her siblings showed support for the new haircut.

Jessa, Jinger’s 28-year-old sister, commented: “Love it!!!”

MOVING FROM MODESTY

The podcast host was raised in a fundamentalist Christian household by her parents Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar who have strict rules for modesty.

Women in their family have not been allowed to wear pants or show their legs in a revealing manner, and typically wear their hair very long down their back.

However, since her brother Josh, 33, was arrested for possession of child porn, Jinger has deviated from the strict lifestyle.

Recently, the young mom was spotted on a shopping trip in Los Angeles wearing short black shorts and a gray tank top.

AWAITING TRIAL

Jinger and Jeremy were supportive when Counting On got axed for another season and was canceled due to her brother Josh’s arrest and child pornography charges.

The oldest Duggar son has been awaiting his trial on home confinement, and if he’s convicted could serve 20 years in prison, with fines up to $250,000 for each count.

Josh’s wife Anna, who is currently pregnant with their seventh child, has stayed strong by her husband’s side despite the shocking charges.

A source previously told The Sun that Anna “has been pointing the finger at everyone else for what has happened to Josh, she’s falling out with family members and isolating herself.”

The couple already share kids Mackynzie, 11, Michael, nine, Maryella, one, Mason, three, Meredith, five, and Marcus, seven. 

‘SPREADING HOPE’

Jinger and Jeremy recently launched a new TikTok page with the intention to “spread hope” after Josh’s arrest.

However, they were quickly slammed by fans when they claimed that there are only “two religions.”

Despite mentioning that there are over 4,200 religions, he then claimed that “when you boil it down, there are actually only two religions.

“One is the religion of man’s achievements and the other is the religion of Jesus’ achievements. The one says you can earn salvation, nirvana, or freedom by what you do.”

“The Bible tells us that no one can be saved by keeping works of the law. But only through faith in Jesus Christ, through faith in what Jesus Christ has done in his life – life, death, and resurrection,” the ex-athlete continued.

“What can you do to obtain your salvation? Or what did Jesus Christ do to obtain salvation for you?” he concluded.

Fans raced to the comments to slam the TV star as they reminded him that there are “thousands of religions” and “Jesus wasn’t even a Christian.”
