Three Interesting Julia Roberts Feet Facts

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 6, 2024

Academy Award winning actress, Julia Roberts, has received a lot of praise throughout her career.  Between her acting abilities and wholesome good looks, it is no surprise that Roberts has been a fan favorite since her breakout role in the 1990 film, Pretty Women.  However, fans’ love for Roberts extends well beyond her acting skills, and her feet have also become a focal point for those who love her most.  Roberts’ feet have been featured all over the Internet and have in turned spark the interest of many.  While most feet receive little to no recognition, Roberts’ famous pair seem to have created a life of their own.  Here are three interesting Julia Roberts feet facts.

3. Julia Roberts doesn’t always have time for pedicures.

Since becoming a mother, Julia Roberts has admitted that her schedule is far more hectic than ever before. As a result, she no longer has the time to pamper her feet regularly.  Although Roberts still makes sure her feet look good for the camera, pedicures have certainly fallen lower on her list of priorities. During an interview, Roberts stated: ” don’t have time to get a pedicure but I sure am happy. Who cares if your feet look bad?”

2. Julia Roberts enjoys being barefoot.

Over the years, there have been several reports of Julia Roberts attending events without any shoes.  In 2013, Roberts decided to go barefoot at the BAFTA awards when she took the stage to present George Clooney with an award. In 2014, Roberts again decided to ditch her shoes when she showed up to HBO’s Emmy Award after party barefoot, her husband, Daniel Moder, carrying her shoes.

1. While on the set of the film Hook, Julia Roberts had an assistant whose sole responsibility was to clean her feet.

While shooting the 1991 film, Hook, Roberts, who played Tinker Bell, had an on-set assistant whose job it was to keep her feet clean and camera ready.
