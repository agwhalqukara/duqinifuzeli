Kevin Smith Shaves His Beard, Is Unrecognizable: Crazy Photo

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Look at this goofy spaz. This is me, beardless, for the @YogaHosers green screen shoot. #FaceBrazilian pic.twitter.com/MZXFJoAoy9

— KevinSmith (@ThatKevinSmith) October 27, 2014

Silent Bob's beard is officially gone! Kevin Smith shaved off his trademark facial fuzz for the first time in over two decades(!) on Monday, Oct. 27, and shared the before-and-after snapshots with his Twitter followers.

"This is my magic beard. Enjoy it now: tomorrow, I am going to shave it off for @YogaHosers. And #CBM is on in 1 hour!" the 44-year-old tweeted on Sunday, Oct. 26.

The film director-writer gave fans two days to brace themselves, but no amount of time could have prepared them for the end result.

"Look at this goofy spa," Smith captioned his newly shaven pic on Oct. 27. "This is me, beardless, for the @YogaHosers green screen shoot. #FaceBrazilian."

The Clerks creator has been rocking facial hair since the early '90s. His other most notable feature? He's known for always wearing an oversized orange and blue hockey jersey.
