Vanessa Hudgens Pets - Celebrity Pets

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Darla (Dog)

Breed: Toy Poodle Mix | Adopted Oct 2016

Vanessa Hudgens has one dog, a toy poodle mix named Darla. While with her then-boyfriend Austin Butler, she adopted Darla from a neighbor of Butler’s mom. His mom noticed the tiny poodle mix sitting on a neighbor’s balcony in the summer heat every day without food or water. She eventually went over to the neighbors to say if they can’t look after the dog, she would. After Butler’s mom passed away, Hudgens and Butler took Darla, with Hudgens keeping Darla after they broke up.

Shadow (Dog)

Breed: Toy Poodle

Vanessa Hudgens used to have a toy poodle named Shadow. Shadow was originally Hudgens’ younger sister’s dog, but she was too young to handle raising her. Hudgens said Shadow chose her to be her mom and looked after her until she passed away in 2016.

Australian Shepherd (Dog)

Breed: Australian Shepherd

Before Cole Tucker and Vanessa Hudgens got married in 2023, Cole had an Australian Shepherd mix.

Vanessa Hudgens

Birthday

December 14, 1988 (35)

Vanessa Hudgens is an American actress and singer. She gained international recognition playing Gabriella Montez in the High School Musical series with Zac Efron and Ashley Tisdale. She has appeared in various Disney specials and series throughout her career. She’s also known for her lead role in The Princess Switch series of movies on Netflix.
