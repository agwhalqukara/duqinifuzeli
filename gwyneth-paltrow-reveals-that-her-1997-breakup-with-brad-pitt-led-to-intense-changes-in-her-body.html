Gwyneth Paltrow Reveals That Her 1997 Breakup With Brad Pitt Led To Intense Changes In Her Body

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 23, 2024

Gwyneth Paltrow has gotten candid about her past relationships before. Fans of the actress will remember when she and Brad Pitt broke up back in 1997. But what they won’t recall is the way the breakup affected her physical and mental health.

In a YouTube video posted on the 10th of January, the 47-year-old actress, in reference to her 1997 appearence on Harper’s Bazaar, began by describing her age at the time, stating that she became visibly skinny on account of not being able to keep food down because she was so upset.

As it was just noted, Paltrow famously graced the cover of Harper’s Bazaar back in 1997 for their December issue, rocking a cropped hair-do. Around the time of the magazine’s publication in the mid-1990s, Pitt and Paltrow were one of the most popular Hollywood couples.

They first started dating after they met on the set of the 1995 movie, Seven. They called off their engagement and marriage in 1997. Even though their breakup was heartbreaking, she and Pitt still get along to this day.

As it was previously reported, the star claimed she and the Once Upon A Time In Hollywood star get along just fine. The two are on great terms.

These days, Gwyneth is married to Brad Falchuk, following her breakup with the frontman of Coldplay, Chris Martin. In past interviews, the star revealed that many of her friends told her that moving into her husband’s home was a terrible decision. However, she did it anyway, despite warnings it may affect their life in the bedroom. 

Gwyneth and Brad got married back in September 2018, and have been together ever since. Chris and Gwyneth, on the other hand, have two children together, including Moses and Apple, 13, and 15, respectively. Chris and Paltrow divorced in 2016.

Brad Pitt, on the other hand, was famously married to Jennifer Aniston for approximately five years, as well as Angelina Jolie. However, Jolie and Pitt broke up back in September 2016 after just two years of holy matrimony.
