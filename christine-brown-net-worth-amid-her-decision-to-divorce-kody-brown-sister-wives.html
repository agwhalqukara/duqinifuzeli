Christine Brown Net Worth - Amid Her Decision To Divorce Kody Brown? Sister Wives

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Christine Brown has finally decided to divorce Kody Brown after 26 years of marriage.

The two were in a special spiritual marriage for so many long years while Kody is legally married to Robyn.

In the show’s new cameo, Christine revealed that the divorce was ‘a long time coming.’

She also expressed that there was ‘sadness,’ but ‘no anger or anything like that, according to Daily Mail UK.

The former spiritual Brown couple is parents to six children; Aspyn, Mykelti, Gwendlyn, Ysabel, Truely, and Paedon.
