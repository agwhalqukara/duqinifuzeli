Five Things You Didnt Know about Dorit Kemsley

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

She’s back, and she’s bringing a friend with! Real Housewife Lisa Vanderpump was unsure whether or not she’d be back for the next season of the Beverly Hills franchise, but it seems that she’s made the decision to come back bigger and better than ever. She’s bringing a friend with her, too; her name is Dorit Kemsley, and you might already know more about her than you think. Now, I know you’re thinking of her name and not recognizing it, but rest assured that she is someone you’d know if you had the chance to get to know her. She’s a designer, and she’s famous in her own right. She’s going to be the newest face in the RHOBH franchise seeing that two faces, including ill supermodel Yolanda Foster, have departed. So, let’s get to know a bit about her before she becomes a household figure.

She’s a newlywed

What we do know about Dorit is that she’s married to Paul Kemsley, who is a very famous man in England. He is also a very wealthy man, and the two of them met in New York City. She lived there, he traveled there often for work. They hit it off and now they are a married couple who recently tied the knot. They’ve only been married about a year and-a-half at this point, exchanging their vows in March 2015.

Her husband is British

Paul Kemsley is a British property developer, and we have a feeling that is how this family knows the Vanderpumps. They are also British, so we have a thought that they might have known one another from their English business ventures. Either way, though, this is a family that is very famous in Britain, and they’re about to become famous here, too.

She’s got a son

Droit and Paul do have a small son together, but we don’t know how old he is precisely. He might be older than their wedding vows, he might not. All we know is that there is a young son in the picture, and that they do share him.

She’s a stepmom

When she married Paul Kemsley, Dorit Kemsley became a stepmother to his three children. Since he was previously married to another English woman for many years before calling it quits with her and moving on to another woman, he does have kids. We have no idea how well they get along with their new stepmother, if the ex-wife is still in the picture in friendly terms or what. We just don’t know.

Her social media profiles no longer exist

When you want to get to know someone, you get to take a closer look at their social media profiles. However, even though she has a Twitter and a Facebook profile both state that they are “No longer available,” and we suspect that they might be removed from the internet since her announcement that she became a new housewife. Perhaps the network wanted to keep her as mysterious as possible so we all want to tune in to the new season to see her?
