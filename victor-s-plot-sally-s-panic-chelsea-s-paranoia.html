Victor's Plot, Sally's Panic, Chelsea's Paranoia

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 8, 2024

The Young and the Restless spoilers tease a week of total freakouts in Genoa City.

The Young and the Restless Spoilers: What Has Victor Done Now?

The Great Victor Newman (Eric Braeden) is determined to protect Nikki Newman (Melody Thomas Scott) from Crazy Aunt Jordan (Colleen Zenk), but he goes to great lengths to do it, and that only serves to drive Victoia Newman (Amelia Heinle) insane. She is just as determined to be protective as Victor, but she is worried about her daughter more than her mother.

Victoria brings her concerns to Cole Howard (J. Eddie Peck), telling him that this time her father has gone too far and Claire Grace might get caught in the crossfire.

Sally’s Success Is A Mess

Sally Spectra (Courtney Hope) realizes everything she has done over the past few months may be for nothing and things are about to blow up in her face. Her interior design business is about to go belly up and she doesn’t know what to do.

Chloe Mitchell Fisher (Elizabeth Hendrickson) is especially distressed to hear this since she left her job at Marchetti to work with Sally and now she might be out of work. Will Adam Newman (Mark Grossman) step in and save the day?

Romance And A Problem Child

Summer Newman (Alison Lanier) and Chance Chancellor (Conner Floyd) are ready for another romantic date at Society, but when Phyllis Summers (Michelle Stafford) sees what is going on she can’t wait to horn in and be her usual obnoxious self. Fortunately for Summer and Chance, Nick Newman (Joshua Morrow) has a little more respect for his adult daughter and tries to get Phyllis to leave the younger adults alone.

Are Summer and Chance about to embark on a full-fledged romance? Plus, what the heck are Phyllis and Nick doing together on what seems like a date?

Finally, Chelsea Lawson (Melissa Claire Egan) is worried sick about Connor Newman (Judah Mackey). She heard from his school and the situation is worse than she ever imagined. When she brings her concerns to Billy Abbott (Jason Thompson), he promises to be there to support her, but what if there is something more wrong with Chelsea’s troubled son than meets the eye?

Tune into The Young and the Restless weekdays on CBS to see how these stories play out.
