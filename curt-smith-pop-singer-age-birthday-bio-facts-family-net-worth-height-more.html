Curt Smith is a singer, songwriter, musician, record producer and co-founder of the pop rock group Tears for Fears with childhood friend Roland Orzabal. Smith has played bass guitar, co-wrote many of the band's songs, accompaniment and lead vocals on hits such as "Mad World", "Pale Shelter", "Change", "The Way You Are" and "Everybody Wants. to Rule the World ". Smith owns a high male voice. After leaving Tears for Fears in 1991, Smith pursued a solo career and released his debut album Soul on Board in 1993. In total, he released five studio albums and one EP, and also tried his hand at acting. He reunited with Orzabal, as Tears for Fears, in 2000 .
