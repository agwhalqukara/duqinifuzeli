Shrinking All Set To Release On Apple Tv+. Trailer Cast &amp; Other Details

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 19, 2024

A bereaved therapist who starts to defy convention and tell his patients what he really feels about them is the focus of the comedy series “Shrinking.”

Release date

On January 27, 2023, the series will be made available on Apple TV+.

Cast and creators

Lukita Maxwell portrays Alice, and Jason Segel plays Jimmy.
Dr. Phil Rhodes is portrayed by Harrison Ford, Christa Miller, and
Sean is Luke Tennie.
As Gaby, Jessica Williams
Bill Lawrence, Brett Goldstein, and Jason Segel are the authors.

Official synopsis

“While also being a dad, friend, and therapist, Jimmy is finding it difficult to cope with the death of his wife. He makes the decision to try a new strategy with everyone who crosses his path: brutal, unfiltered honesty. Can he help others while also helping himself? Will it reintroduce him to the light?

Other details

On Friday, January 27, the first two episodes of Shrinking will be released worldwide. Thereafter, a brand-new episode will air every Friday.

Teaser

The teaser for the show doesn’t give many plot details, despite the presence of Harrison Ford, Jason Segel, and other series characters.
