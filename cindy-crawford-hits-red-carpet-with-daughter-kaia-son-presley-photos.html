Cindy Crawford Hits Red Carpet With Daughter Kaia, Son Presley: Photos

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Family time! 

Cindy Crawford made it a Gerber date night on Thursday, Feb. 5, for the screening of Hospital in the Sky in New York City. In tow were the supermodel’s entrepreneur husband Rande Gerber, aspiring model daughter Kaia, and her rising photographer son Presley — and clearly, good looks run in the family!  

The look-alike quartet looked radiant on the red carpet. While Gerber, 52, sported a black leather jacket, a denim shirt, and jeans, and the 49-year-old legendary model looked striking in a navy wrap dress with knee-high boots, their beautiful children stole the spotlight.

Kaia, 13, who is a spitting image of her famous supermodel mom, stunned in a bedazzled top with a black pencil skirt and leather booties. Seriously, from her gorgeous wavy brown tresses to her porcelain skin, the teen really lucked out in the genetics department. Presley, 15, took a night off from his photography sessions, and stepped in front of the lens in a blue sweatshirt, a black puffer coat, and denim jeans.

In June 2014, the couple’s children teamed up for a photography project that sometimes called for Kaia to raid her mother’s closet for designer wear. The amazing photo shoots eventually landed Kaia a feature in Teen Vogue. 

The event was in celebration of Orbis International, a nonprofit organization that works to prevent and treat blindness around the world. “I loved the idea that not only do they go in and provide much needed eye care for places, but they also teach the local doctors, so once they leave there’s a program in place so their work can continue,” Crawford told Us Weekly.

“This journey was about me learning about Orbis and just celebrating what they do because so much of what they do, all the money is donated, and then all the doctors and nurses are volunteer. It’s really an incredible organization.”
